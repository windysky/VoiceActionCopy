VoiceClip — Important Information

Privacy
- VoiceClip uses Windows' built-in Online Speech Recognition (the same service Voice Access uses).
- Recognized text is stored ONLY on your computer (clipboard and local history file).
- VoiceClip itself makes no network calls and sends nothing to the developer.
- Voice audio handling is governed by Microsoft's Online Speech Recognition privacy policy.

- You are responsible for reviewing dictated text before relying on it.

Requirements
- Windows 11 (build 22621 or later).
- Windows Online Speech Recognition must be enabled
  (Settings -> Privacy & security -> Speech).
- A working microphone.
